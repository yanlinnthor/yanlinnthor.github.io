Digital Ocean Account Creation

yan April 4, 2020

Just a simple guide to assist non-tech people.

1. Register at

- DigitalOcean - The developer cloud

2. Enter billing info with your credit card

3. Set up team account

- How to Create Teams | DigitalOcean Product Documentation

- Do not need to check "Convert your account" into a team.

- Do not need to check "Secure sign-in"

4. Invite team members

- enter the emails in the textarea

- click Invite

- Invited user has to accept the invitation via email

5. Give the team member necessary role

How to Manage Team Membership | DigitalOcean Product Documentation

- usually "Member" role is enough
